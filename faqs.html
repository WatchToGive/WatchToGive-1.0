Frequently Asked Questions

How does Watch To Give work?

You simply watch ads on our platform, and the revenue generated from the ads is donated to charities on your behalf.

Is it really free?

Yes, it’s completely free! The donations come from advertisers who pay for your attention. Your time is the only contribution needed.

Which charities do you support?

We partner with a variety of registered charities focused on causes like hunger relief, education, and medical support. You can find more details on our About Us page.
